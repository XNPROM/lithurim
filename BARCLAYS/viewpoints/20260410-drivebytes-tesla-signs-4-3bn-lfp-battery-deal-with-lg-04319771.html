Tesla, Inc.

DriveBytes: Tesla signs $4.3bn LFP battery deal with LG

3-year agreement starting in 2027. Cells to be made in Michigan at former GM/LG Ultium plant and will supply Tesla's Megapack 3 ESS produced in Houston. Adds to Tesla's current LFP cell capacity from Gigafactory Nevada, and reported $1bn+ deal for LFP cells from Samsung/STLA JV plant also in 2027
