## 核心观点 / Key Takeaways
1. 继续看好锂电池材料的投资机遇，预计相关产业链公司将迎来产品量价齐升，带动盈利上行。We remain optimistic about investment opportunities in lithium battery materials, with related companies expected to see volume and price increases, driving profitability.
2. 储能需求高增将带动锂电池需求增长，2026年全球储能新增装机量预计同比增长87%。High growth in energy storage demand will drive lithium battery demand, with global new energy storage installations expected to increase by 87% YoY in 2026.
3. 中东冲突正由化工原料向锂电池材料产业链传导价格影响，电解液溶剂、针状焦、硫磺等原材料价格上涨。The Middle East conflict is transmitting price impacts from chemical raw materials to the lithium battery materials chain, with prices of electrolyte solvents, needle coke, sulfur, and other raw materials rising.
4. 钠电池产业化有望加速，在碳酸锂价格处于阶段性高位的背景下，国内储能及入门级电动车受成本影响推动钠电池发展. Sodium battery industrialization is expected to accelerate, with domestic energy storage and entry-level electric vehicles driven by cost factors amid high lithium carbonate prices.

## 风险提示 / Risk Factors
1. 政策性风险：全球碳中和背景下各国政策变化可能带来估值回落。Policy risks: Changes in global carbon neutrality policies could lead to valuation adjustments.
2. 原材料价格大幅波动：多晶硅料和碳酸锂价格波动可能影响企业经营业绩。Significant fluctuations in raw material prices: Price volatility of polysilicon and lithium carbonate could impact corporate performance.
3. 下游需求不及预期：新能源和电动车需求若边际弱化将影响企业盈利和估值. Downstream demand underperformance: Weakening marginal demand for new energy and electric vehicles would affect corporate profitability and valuations.
