## 核心观点 / Key Takeaways
1. 2025年第四季度净利润低于预期，主要原因是储能系统盈利能力波动和公司激励成本增加。
   Q4 2025 net profit was below expectations, mainly due to profitability fluctuations in energy storage systems and increased corporate incentive costs.
   
2. 2025年公司核心业务出货表现良好，全球领先地位稳固，包括光伏逆变器、储能系统、氢能和风电变流器。
   The company's core business showed strong shipment performance in 2025 with its global leading position maintained across photovoltaic inverters, energy storage systems, hydrogen energy, and wind power converters.
   
3. 2026年有望在AI数据中心业务实现突破，看好储能和AI需求带来的长期成长性。
   The company is expected to achieve breakthroughs in AI data center business in 2026, with long-term growth potential driven by energy storage and AI demand.
