## 核心观点 / Key Takeaways
1. 宁德时代在"超级科技日"上发布了多款技术领先产品，包括第三代神行超充电池、第三代麒麟电池、钠新电池以及充电网络建设计划，展示了其长期研发投入带来的产品多元布局及性能优势。
   CATL unveiled several technologically advanced products at its "Super Technology Day," including the third-generation Shenxing ultra-fast charging battery, third-generation Kirin battery, sodium-ion battery, and charging network expansion plans, demonstrating its diversified product portfolio and performance advantages from long-term R&D investment.

2. 野村东方国际证券上调公司2026-2028年动力电池和储能电池平均售价预测，上调营收预测，但下调毛利率预测，主要考虑到碳酸锂价格上行可能侵蚀产品盈利能力。
   Nomura Oriental International Securities has upgraded the average selling price forecasts for CATL's power and energy storage batteries for 2026-2028, as well as revenue forecasts, but lowered gross margin forecasts primarily due to potential margin erosion from rising lithium carbonate prices.

3. 野村东方国际证券将估值基准切换至2027年，维持增持评级，上调目标价至612元，隐含40.3%的上行空间。
   Nomura Oriental International Securities has switched its valuation benchmark to 2027, maintained its "Add" rating, and raised the target price to 612 yuan, implying an upside potential of 40.3%.
