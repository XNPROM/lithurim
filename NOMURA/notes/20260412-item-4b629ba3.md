## 核心观点 / Key Takeaways
1. 户储需求前景看好：即使中东冲突缓和，以光伏/储能为代表的分布式能源仍有望成为新一轮能源安全诉求的优先选项，中国产业链公司具有中长期投资价值。
   Residential energy storage demand outlook remains positive: Even if the Middle East conflict eases, distributed energy represented by photovoltaics and energy storage is expected to remain a priority for new energy security demands, with Chinese companies offering long-term investment value.

2. 电力设备出海机遇：看好AI基建类产业链重新受益于行业趋势演进，叠加4月业绩潜在超预期利好。
   Power equipment export opportunities: The AI infrastructure industry chain is expected to benefit from industry trends again, with potential earnings surprises in April adding to the positive outlook.

3. 储能和锂电池板块表现强劲：本周储能指数、锂电池指数表现相对较好，涨幅分别为8.97%、8.90%。
   Strong performance in energy storage and lithium battery sectors: This week, the energy storage index and lithium battery index performed relatively well, with increases of 8.97% and 8.90% respectively.

4. 3月电池数据亮眼：2026年3月中国动力电池销量同比增长31.1%，储能电池销量同比增长115.9%。
   Impressive March battery data: In March 2026, China's power battery sales increased by 31.1% year-on-year, while energy storage battery sales increased by 115.9% year-on-year.

5. 3月储能招标数据大幅增长：2026年3月国内储能EPC、储能系统、储能电芯新增采招落地规模共计13GW/81.9GWh，同比增长54.5%(功率)/138%(容量)。
   Significant growth in March energy storage bidding: In March 2026, the scale of new domestic energy storage EPC, systems, and cell procurement reached 13GW/81.9GWh, year-on-year increases of 54.5%(power)/138%(capacity).

## 关键数据 / Key Data
- 申万电力设备指数本周上涨6.98%，年初至今累计上涨9.57%
- 光伏产业链价格：多晶硅料价格33-35元/kg，硅片价格0.93-1.23元/片，电池片价格0.34-0.35元/W，组件报价0.76-0.80元/W
- 锂电产业链价格：碳酸锂价格震荡下行，三元材料价格小幅下降，磷酸铁锂价格下降约700元/吨
- 电力设备PE(2026年4月10日)为41.05倍，估值分位位于十年来的65.51%分位
- 推荐股票：德业股份(605117 CH)、锦浪科技(300763 CH)、思源电气(002028 CH)、金盘科技(688676 CH)，评级均为"增持"

## 投资建议 / Investment Recommendation
增持 (Buy)
推荐德业股份(605117 CH)、锦浪科技(300763 CH)作为户储产业链标的，建议关注正泰电源、固德威。
推荐思源电气(002028 CH)、金盘科技(688676 CH)作为AI基建和电力设备出海标的，建议关注伊戈尔、中恒电气。

## 风险提示 / Risk Factors
1. 政策性风险：全球碳中和背景下各国政策变化可能带来估值回落。
   Policy risks: Changes in global carbon neutrality policies could lead to valuation adjustments.

2. 原材料价格大幅波动：多晶硅料和碳酸锂价格波动可能影响企业经营业绩。
   Raw material price volatility: Significant fluctuations in polysilicon and lithium carbonate prices could affect corporate performance.

3. 下游需求不及预期：新能源和电动车需求若边际弱化将影响企业盈利和估值。
   Downstream demand underperformance: If the growth of new energy and electric vehicle demand slows, it will impact corporate profitability and valuations.
