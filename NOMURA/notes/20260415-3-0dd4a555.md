## 报告主体 / Report Subject
野村国际（Nomura）宏观点评：2026年3月中国出口增长放缓而进口提速

## 核心观点 / Key Takeaways
1. 3月中国出口同比增长显著放缓至2.5%，远低于市场预期，主要受春节错位及高基数影响；进口同比增长则加速至27.8%，超出市场预期，主要受半导体价格上涨驱动。
   In March, China's export growth significantly slowed to 2.5% year-on-year, well below market expectations, mainly affected by the Spring Festival timing shift and high base effect; import growth accelerated to 27.8% year-on-year, exceeding market expectations, primarily driven by semiconductor price increases.

2. 尽管整体出口放缓，中国在可再生能源相关产品（电动汽车、锂电池、风电设备）出口方面表现强劲，显示出在这些领域的竞争优势和能源安全优势。
   Despite the overall slowdown in exports, China showed strong performance in exporting renewable energy-related products (electric vehicles, lithium-ion batteries, wind power equipment), demonstrating competitive advantages and energy security strengths in these sectors.

3. 一季度中国对主要出口目的地（包括美国、欧盟、日本、韩国及东盟）的出口增速均较四季度有所改善，显示出出口动能的恢复。
   In Q1, China's export growth to major destinations (including the US, EU, Japan, South Korea, and ASEAN) all improved compared to Q4, indicating a recovery in export momentum.

4. 进口异常增长主要由半导体（尤其是存储芯片）价格飙升驱动，集成电路进口额增速远高于进口量增速；大宗商品进口量增速呈现分化。
   The abnormal import growth was mainly driven by soaring semiconductor prices (especially memory chips), with the growth rate of integrated circuit import value significantly higher than import volume; import volume growth for major commodities showed divergence.

## 关键数据 / Key Data
- 3月出口同比增速：2.5%（1-2月：21.8%；市场预期：8.6%）
- 3月进口同比增速：27.8%（1-2月：19.8%；市场预期：13.9%）
- 3月贸易顺差：511亿美元（1-2月月均：1068亿美元）
- 3月集成电路出口同比增速：85.2%
- 3月汽车（含底盘）出口同比增速：43.9%
- 3月自动数据处理设备及零部件出口同比增速：37.2%
- 3月服装、鞋靴、箱包及玩具出口同比增速分别为：-29.4%、-39.6%、-34.5%和-41.9%
