## 核心观点 / Key Takeaways
1. 科达利2026年一季度业绩超预期，营收41.4亿元（同比增长37.1%），归母净利润4.6亿元（同比增长19.1%）。Kedaili's Q1 2026 results exceeded expectations, with revenue of 4.14 billion yuan (up 37.1% YoY) and net profit of 460 million yuan (up 19.1% YoY).

2. 一季度毛利率环比下降3.8个百分点至21.2%，主要反映部分客户传递的价格压力，但全年仍看好通过高价值量新品和海外工厂产能提升带动增长。 Q1 gross margin decreased by 3.8 percentage points to 21.2% mainly due to price pressure from some customers, but we remain optimistic about full-year growth driven by high-value new products and increased utilization of overseas factory capacity.

3. 野村上调公司2026-2028年营收预测12%，下调毛利率预测，但上调净利润预测和目标价至293.04元，隐含42.8%上行空间。 Nomura raised the company's 2026-2028 revenue forecast by 12%, lowered gross margin forecast, but increased net profit forecast and target price to 293.04 yuan, implying 42.8% upside potential.
