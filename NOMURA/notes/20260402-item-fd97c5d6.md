## 核心观点 / Key Takeaways
1. 先导智能2025年净利润表现符合预期，锂电池设备营收同比增长23.2%，反映出公司充分受益于下游需求修复。XIAOMAI Intelligent's 2025 net profit met expectations, with lithium battery equipment revenue growing 23.2% YoY, reflecting the company's full benefit from downstream demand recovery.

2. 公司在新兴赛道产品持续突破，包括固态电池设备、钠离子电池设备、储能电池设备和光伏设备，支撑公司未来成长性。 The company has achieved continuous breakthroughs in emerging track products, including solid-state battery equipment, sodium-ion battery equipment, energy storage battery equipment, and photovoltaic equipment, supporting the company's future growth.

3. 野村东方国际证券维持增持评级，上调目标价至73元，隐含50.8%的上行空间，看好公司在全球储能需求景气以及全固态电池产业化持续推进背景下的增长潜力。 Nomura maintains its Buy rating and raises the target price to RMB 73, implying 50.8% upside potential, expressing optimism about the company's growth prospects amid global energy storage demand and solid-state battery commercialization.
