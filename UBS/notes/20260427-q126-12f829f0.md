## 核心观点 / Key Takeaways
1. **业绩低于预期**：赢合科技2026年第一季度收入20.5亿元（环比下降23%），归母净利润6200万元（环比下降74%），业绩低于瑞银预期，Q1利润仅占瑞银2026年盈利预测的11%。
   *Win Technology's Q1 2026 revenue was RMB 2.05 billion (down 23% QoQ), with net profit of RMB 62 million (down 74% QoQ), falling below UBS expectations. Q1 profit accounted for only 11% of UBS's 2026 profit forecast.*

2. **毛利率大幅下滑**：公司Q1毛利率为18.2%（同比下降10个百分点，环比下降9个百分点)，主要受电子烟和锂电设备业务利润贡献下降影响。
   *The company's Q1 gross margin was 18.2% (down 10 YoY and 9 QoQ), mainly affected by declining profit contributions from its e-cigarette and lithium battery equipment businesses.*

3. **电子烟业务表现疲软**：电子烟业务贡献约1300万元（归母口径），同环比下滑82%/58%，成为业绩拖累因素。
   *The e-cigarette business contributed approximately RMB 13 million (on a net profit basis), down 82% YoY and 58% QoQ, becoming a drag on performance.*

4. **锂电设备业务利润率下降**：锂电设备业务利润贡献约5000万元，对应净利率约3%（2025年为4%），显示盈利能力有所减弱。
   *The lithium battery equipment business contributed approximately RMB 50 million in profit, with a net margin of about 3% (compared to 4% in 2025), indicating weakened profitability.*

5. **投资者预期负面反应**：公司未提供新的业务指引，瑞银预期投资者反应将偏负面。
   *The company did not provide new guidance, and UBS expects a negative reaction from investors.*

## 风险提示 / Risk Factors

2. 新能源汽车销量弱于预期和下游产能扩张可能导致设备需求下降
3. 电池设备领域竞争加剧，可能显著降低利润率
