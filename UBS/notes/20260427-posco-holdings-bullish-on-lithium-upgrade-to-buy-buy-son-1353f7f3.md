## 核心观点 / Key Takeaways
1. UBS将POSCO评级从中性上调至买入，目标价从320,000韩元上调至500,000韩元，主要看好其锂资产，特别是阿根廷业务的盈利前景。
   UBS upgrades POSCO to Buy from Neutral with a price target raised to Won500,000 from Won320,000, primarily due to optimism about its lithium assets, especially the profitability prospects of its Argentina operation.

2. POSCO阿根廷业务预计在2026年实现盈亏平衡，随着二期扩建完成，产能将增至5万吨/年，三期扩建计划于2029年完成。
   POSCO's Argentina operation is expected to break even in 2026, with capacity increasing to 50ktpa after Phase 2 completion, and Phase 3 expansion planned for 2029.

3. POSCO与JSW在印度的合资建厂被视为积极战略举措，将受益于印度长期结构性钢铁需求增长。
   POSCO's JV with JSW to build a blast furnace in India is viewed as a positive strategic move that will benefit from India's long-term structural steel demand growth.

4. 尽管预计2026年第一季度业绩将不及市场预期，但UBS认为任何股价回调都是吸引人的买入机会。
   Although 1Q26 results are expected to miss market expectations, UBS believes any resulting share price correction could present an attractive buying opportunity.

## 风险提示 / Risk Factors
1. 中国钢铁出口可能限制钢铁利润的上行空间。
   Chinese steel exports could cap the upside for steel profits.

2. 2026年第一季度业绩可能不及市场预期，运营利润可能比共识低9%。
   1Q26 results may miss market expectations, with operating profit potentially 9% below consensus.

3. 锂业务中的PPLS(矿石)业务预计在2029年前将持续亏损，因为高锂辉石价格的影响。
   The PPLS (ore) business within lithium operations is expected to remain loss-making until 2029 due to high spodumene prices.

4. 伊朗冲突相关影响以及钢铁市场疲软可能对短期业绩造成压力。
   Impacts from the Iran conflict and a weaker steel outlook may pressure near-term performance.
