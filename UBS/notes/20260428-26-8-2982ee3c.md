## 核心观点 / Key Takeaways
1. 三美股份2026年一季度净利润同比/环比增长26%/8%至5.06亿元，但低于UBS预期，主要因制冷剂销量同比/环比下滑7%/15%。
   / Sanmei Co. Ltd.'s Q1 2026 net profit increased 26%/8% YoY/QoQ to Rmb506 million, below UBS expectations, mainly due to refrigerant sales declining 7%/15% YoY/QoQ.

2. 一季度制冷剂销量环比下降15%，但销售均价环比上涨15%，其中R22/R32/R125/R134a国内平均价格环比+0%/+0%/12%/5%。
   / Q1 refrigerant sales volume decreased 15% QoQ, while average selling price increased 15% QoQ, with domestic average prices for R22/R32/R125/R134a up 0%/0%/12%/5% QoQ.

3. 公司稳步推进含氟材料项目：1,500吨/年六氟磷酸锂产能处于技改阶段；5,000吨/年聚全氟乙丙烯项目处于设备仪表调试阶段；5,000吨/年聚偏氟乙烯项目处于设备安装阶段。
   / The company is steadily advancing fluorinated materials projects: 1,500 tons/year lithium hexafluorophosphate capacity is in technical modification stage; 5,000 tons/year polytetrafluoroethylene project is in equipment commissioning stage; 5,000 tons/year polyvinylidene fluoride project is in equipment installation stage.

4. 二季度以来，主流制冷剂价格继续上涨，R22/R32/R125/R134a平均价格环比+7%/1%/7%/4%，主要受旺季需求回升叠加成本（硫酸价格）上涨驱动。
   / Since Q2, mainstream refrigerant prices have continued to rise, with average prices for R22/R32/R125/R134a up 7%/1%/7%/4% QoQ, mainly driven by seasonal demand recovery and rising costs (sulfuric acid prices).

## 风险提示 / Risk Factors
1. 制冷剂下游需求受到宏观经济影响，存在不确定性
   / Downstream demand for refrigerants is subject to uncertainty due to macroeconomic factors

2. 三代制冷剂配额分配和调整存在不确定性
   / Uncertainty in allocation and adjustment of third-generation refrigerant quotas

3. 四代制冷剂替代三代制冷剂进度可能超预期
   / The substitution of fourth-generation refrigerants for third-generation refrigerants may happen faster than expected

4. 海外PFAS监管趋严可能对制冷剂出口造成不利影响
   / Tightening overseas PFAS regulations may adversely affect refrigerant exports
