瑞银(UBS)主题研究报告：电池超级周期推动新能源与电动汽车发展

## 核心观点 / Key Takeaways
1. 电池技术正迎来汽车和电力领域的明显转折点，瑞银拆解分析显示电动汽车在成本、续航和充电方面已接近全面超越传统燃油车，采用模式正从政策驱动转向消费者主导。
   Battery technology is reaching a clear inflection point across autos and power. UBS teardown analysis shows EVs approaching full "triple parity" with ICEs on cost, range and charging, shifting adoption from policy-driven to consumer-led.

2. 中国在电池制造和整车领域保持约30-35%的结构性成本优势，尽管贸易壁垒上升，但仍支持其持续扩大全球市场份额，并加速了储能系统(BESS)的采用，使其成为电动汽车后最大的收入池，具有更高的增长和利润潜力。
   Cost leadership remains concentrated in China, where battery makers and OEMs retain a ~30–35% structural advantage, supporting ongoing global share gains despite rising trade barriers. These gains are accelerating BESS adoption, now the largest revenue pool after EVs with superior growth and margin potential.

3. 美国在储能部署方面处于领先地位，政策明确、税收优惠丰厚且电池产能过剩，能够实现快速规模化，到2030年储能需求预计将增长约三倍至约200GWh。
   The US is leading deployment momentum, with US policy clarity, generous tax credits and surplus battery capacity enabling rapid scale-up. BESS demand is set to roughly triple to ~200 GWh by 2030.

## 关键数据 / Key Data
- 韩国电池制造商预计到2030年将占据美国约85%的储能市场份额
- Korean battery makers are positioned to capture ~85% of US BESS market share by 2030E
- 瑞银分析显示磷酸铁锂电池(LFP)成本远低于市场共识，推动系统成本下降30%
- UBS analysis sees onshore lithium-iron phosphate (LFP) battery costs well below consensus, driving a 30% drop in system costs
- 政策支持可覆盖高达50%的资本支出，实现两位数的内部收益率(IRR)
- With policy support covering up to 50% of capex and double-digit IRRs
