### 2026-04-28 | UBS | Iron Ore & Coal "What's new? BHP-CMRG agreement; Indonesian 

## 报告主体 / Report Subject
瑞银集团 (UBS) 铁矿石与煤炭研究报告，分析师 Myles Allsop，发布日期 2026年4月27日。

## 核心观点 / Key Takeaways
1. BHP-CMRG协议：必和必拓(BHP)与中国宝武钢铁集团(CMRG)达成新合约，标志着全球大宗商品定价可能发生转变。该协议包括对部分产品提供1.8%的折扣，并停止使用普氏(Platts)指数，转而采用包括北京铁矿石交易中心(COREX)在内的混合定价公式。此举可能成为其他矿商的参考点。
   
   BHP-CMRG Agreement: BHP has reached a new agreement with China Baowu Steel Group (CMRG), potentially signaling a shift in global commodity pricing. The deal includes a 1.8% discount on some products and abandonment of the Platts index in favor of a mixed pricing formula including Beijing Iron Ore Trading Centre (COREX). This could serve as a reference point for other miners.

2. 印尼煤炭配额：印尼能源与矿产资源部将2026年煤炭产量配额下调至约6亿吨(较2025年实际产量7.9亿吨下降25%)。但据报道，总统已要求释放更多煤炭开采配额，考虑到该国不断扩大的财政赤字和疲软的货币，这一调整可能性较大。
   
   Indonesian Coal Quotas: Indonesia's Ministry of Energy & Mineral Resources has cut the 2026 coal output quota to ~600Mt (25% lower than 2025's actual production of 790Mt). However, the President has reportedly requested more coal mining quotas to be released, which is likely given the country's widening fiscal deficit and weakening currency.

3. 铁矿石市场：铁矿石价格保持坚挺在108美元/吨，尽管中东冲突影响有限。2026年铁矿石发货量强劲，需求基本稳定，导致库存增加。中国港口铁矿石库存接近历史高点约1.6亿吨，但钢厂和贸易商库存处于相对正常水平。
   
   Iron Ore Market: Iron ore prices remain resilient at $108/t with limited impact from the Middle East conflict. Iron ore shipments have been strong in 2026 with stable demand, leading to inventory buildup. China's port iron ore inventories are near an all-time high at ~160Mt, while steel mill and trader inventories are at relatively normal levels.
